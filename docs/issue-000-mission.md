# Mission and Vision

## AI Investment Journey

### Mission

To provide educational resources and practical tools that democratize understanding of advanced investment analysis techniques, particularly through the lens of machine learning and explainability.

### Vision

- Build a comprehensive collection of accessible learning materials
- Bridge the gap between data science and investment management
- Foster a community of practitioners interested in AI-driven investment strategies
- Maintain high quality and rigor in explanations and implementations

### Core Principles

1. **Clarity**: Complex concepts explained accessibly
2. **Rigor**: Sound mathematical and statistical foundations
3. **Practicality**: Immediately applicable examples
4. **Community**: Open to contributions and feedback
5. **Reproducibility**: All code and analyses should be reproducible
