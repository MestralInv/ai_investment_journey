# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and professional
- Welcome feedback and ideas
- Focus on collaborative problem-solving
- Respect diverse viewpoints and experiences

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it respectfully and constructively.

## Enforcement

Violations will be addressed promptly and fairly.
